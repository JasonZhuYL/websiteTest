Bio and Research

Yuliang is a PhD student at the Computational Sensing and Imaging Lab at Imperial College London, where his research focuses on hardware development for the Unlimited Sensing Framework.

He completed both his undergraduate and master's degrees in Electrical and Information Engineering at Imperial College London, graduating with first-class honors in 2023. Yuliang was also awarded the prestigious Eric Laithwaite Prize, which recognizes his exceptional innovation in his master's project.
